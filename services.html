Ocean Freight

Exit Air Cargo LLC, leveraging its extensive global network, is strategically equipped to manage every stage of your shipment from origin to destination. Our comprehensive suite of ocean freight solutions includes Ocean Forwarding, Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) Consolidation, Multi-Country Consolidation, and integrated Sea/Air services—ensuring flexibility and efficiency tailored to your logistics needs.

  • Port to Port
  • LCL Consolidation
  • Export/Import Documentation
  • Cargo Tracking & Tracing
  • Sea - Air Shipment
  • FCL - Import and Export
  • Sea - Ro – Ro
